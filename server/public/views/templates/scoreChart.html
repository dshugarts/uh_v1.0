How Do I Earn Points?

Age:

Low Risk (+5 Points): Men under 46 and Women under 56

High Risk (+0 Points): Men over 45 and Women over 55

Nicotine:

Low Risk (+10 Points): Not a Nicotine User

High Risk (+0 Points): Nicotine User

ACSM Risk Questionnaire:

Low Risk (+10 Points): Answered No to all questions

High Risk (+0 Points): Answered Yes to 1 or more questions

Family History:

Low Risk (+5 Points): Father NOT diagnosed with CVD before 55 and Mother NOT diagnosed with CVD before 65

High Risk (+0 Points): Father diagnosed with CVD before 55 or Mother diagnosed with CVD before 65

Physical Activity:

Low Risk (+16 Points): Moderate physical activity over 149 min/week

Low Risk (+12 Points): Moderate physical activity between 120-149 min/week

Moderate Risk (+8 Points): Moderate physical activity between 90-119 min/week

Moderate Risk (+4 Points): Moderate physical activity between 60-89 min/week

High Risk (+0 Points): Moderate physical activity under 60 min/week

Physical Inactivity:

Low Risk (+12 Points): 60 minutes or less of continuous sitting at a time, in a given day

Moderate Risk (+6 Point): Between 61-89 minutes of continuous sitting at a time, in a given day

Moderate Risk (+3 Point): Between 90-119 minutes of continuous sitting at a time, in a given day

High Risk (+0 Points): 120 minutes or more of continuous sitting at a time, in a given day

Blood Pressure:

Low Risk (+12 Points): Systolic below 121 mmHg and Diastolic below 81 mmHg

Moderate Risk (+8 Point): Systolic 121-129 mmHg and Diastolic below 81 mmHg

Moderate Risk (+4 Point): Systolic 130-139 mmHg and Diastolic 81-89 mmHg

High Risk (+0 Points): Systolic 140-179 mmHg and Diastolic 90-119 mmHg

Stress:

Low Risk (+10 Points): Total Stress rated 0-2 (out of 5)

Moderate Risk (+6 Points): Total Stress rated 3-5 (out of 5) and Stress Management rated 4-5 (out of 5)

Moderate Risk (+3 Points): Total Stress rated 3-5 (out of 5) and Stress Management rated 3 (out of 5)

High Risk (+0 Points): Total Stress rated 3-5 (out of 5) and Stress Management rated 0-2 (out of 5)

Waist Size (in.):

Low Risk (+10 Points): Men under 40in. and Women under 35in.

Moderate Risk (+6 Points): Men between 40-44in. and Women between 35-39in.

Moderate Risk (+3 Points): Men under 45-49in. and Women between 40-44in.

High Risk (+0 Points): Men over 49in. and Women over 44in.

Sleep:

Low Risk (+10 Points): Sleeping 7+ hours/night

Moderate Risk (+4 Points): Sleeping 5-7 hours/night

High Risk (+0 Points): Sleeping under 5 hours/night
